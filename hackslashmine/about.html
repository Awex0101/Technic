Reimagine Minecraft as the greatest 3D action-RPG platform the world has to offer, and you've got the basis for Hack/Mine. Crawl through completely randomized dungeons with your friends for loads of magical loot! Slaughter hordes of new monsters with tons o' entirely customizable spells! Explore an infinite number of randomly-generated, multi-chunk Areas as one of three classes and four races! Explore a massive, breath-taking new dimension! And finally, reinvent your entire experience with Spellscript, a scripting language for Minecraft that gives you boundless control over the innards of the game.
